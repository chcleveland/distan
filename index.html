Distan

A strategy game about school-district consolidation and expansion, played in the style of Settlers of Catan. Compete to grow the strongest network of schools by collecting enrollment, funding, teachers, facilities, and supplies across the county — then consolidate small schools into full district campuses. First to 10 victory points wins.

New game

How to play

Each hex is a community that produces one resource when its number is rolled on two dice. Your buildings sit on the corners where communities meet, so a school touching three communities collects from all three.

Resources

  • 🎓 Enrollment students who fill your seats
  • 💰 Funding local tax & state dollars
  • 🍎 Teachers certified staff
  • 🏗️ Facilities land & construction
  • 📚 Supplies books & materials

Key rules

  • Roll 7 and the budget cut moves: anyone holding more than 7 cards discards half, and you steal a resource from a district touching the cut hex.
  • Longest bus network (5+ connected routes) and strongest administration (3+ Administrator policy cards) are each worth 2 VP.
  • Trade with the bank at 4:1, or better if you build a school on a trading port.
  • Place your first two schools during setup — the second one collects starting resources.
